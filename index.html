About us

The Studio is a flexible space in 208b Butler Library that supports a range of events and activities for up to 40 people. The space is scheduled and managed by the Center for Teaching and Learning and the Digital Scholarship unit at Columbia University Libraries. To learn more about our partners visit our Partners page. Members of the Columbia community may request use of the Studio for an educational or digital scholarship project. To do so send us a line. To receive updates sign up to our mailing list.
